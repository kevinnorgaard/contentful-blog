Hi, I'm Áine [Aun-yuh]. Áine Carina. I'm an artist, traveler, girly-girl, a bit of a control freak, and creator of Carina Collective. For me, that means sharing tactics on approaches to alternative health and wellness, shopping, life hacks, and being an artist.
